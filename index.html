Essential accessories for adventurous cats

We also really enjoy walking outdoors, but safety must always come first. For this, it is best to choose calm places, without many people and preferably in nature. Furthermore, with the right accessories, our walkss will be safer and more fun.

Carrier

The carrier is used for travel, but also as a safe place if the cat feels uncomfortable.

Collar

A collar with safety breakaway and a identification plate with the owner's details.

Harness

A harness will allow you to use the leash comfortably.

Extendable leash

A leash gives the cat some freedom to explore.
